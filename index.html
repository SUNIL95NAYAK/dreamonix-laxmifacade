FAQ

Common Questions

What types of glazing systems do you install?
We install structural glazing, spider glazing, curtain wall unitized/stick systems, skylight glazing, and point-fixed glass facades. We work with tempered, laminated, double-glazed, and low-e glass units depending on project requirements.
Do you provide a warranty on facade installations?
Yes. We provide a 5-year workmanship warranty on all installations and pass through the manufacturer's warranty on materials, which is typically 10–15 years for quality aluminum systems and glass units.
Can you work with our architect's existing design drawings?
Absolutely. Our engineering team works directly from architect-provided GFCs, AutoCAD drawings, or BIM files to produce accurate shop drawings and fabrication specs aligned with your design intent.
What is the typical project timeline for a medium-sized facade?
A medium commercial building (5–8 floors) typically takes 8–14 weeks from design sign-off to installation completion. Timeline depends on facade complexity, material availability, and site readiness.
Do you handle projects outside Odisha?
Yes. We have executed projects across Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, West Bengal, and Jharkhand. We welcome inquiries from across India and can deploy mobile site teams with our own supervision.
How do I get a quote for my project?
Simply fill out the contact form on this page or call us directly. Share your drawings, site dimensions, or even rough sketches and we'll get back to you with a detailed preliminary estimate within 48 hours.
